METAL MAN

Metal Man Quick Man Bubble Man Wood Man Air Man Heat Man Crash Man Flash Man




TIPS

  • Use metal Blade (his own weapon) to quickly take him out
  • Using the MEGA BUSTER geting at a medium distance jumping over his blades and shoot him as he jumps
  • A conveyer belt in the stage changes directions position yourself in a way the it moves away from metal man
  • Dont get too close as he will try to jump and trap you.
  • When metal Man jumps high he will throw three blades which are easily dodgeable
  • Standing at the edge will make him jump giving you a clear shot.

ROLE

  • He is a combat robot created by DR. Willy
  • He is known for being fast, and combat focused
  • The way to beat him is strategically as he is a challenging opponent
  • Most of the enemies in his stage are all drill based

ABILITIES

  • metal Blade Throw: Large saw blades that come at you from multiple direction
  • High Mobility: He can jump frequently and can move quickly across the arena.
  • Contact Damage: Deals 6 damage on direct contact with Mega Man
  • Weakness: Is very vulnerable to his own weapon
