TIPS

  • Molten lava kills instantly if you touch it without invincibility.
  • Bottomless pits are common; time your jumps carefully.
  • Disappearing blocks (like in Ice Man’s stage) require precise timing to cross safely StrategyWiki+1.
  • Springers and Telly enemies can block your path or damage you if you’re not ready

ROLE

  • High heat resistance to fire and magma megaman.fandom.com+1.s
  • Atomic Fire as his Special Weapon, capable of bursts up to 12,000°C (21,632°F) megaman.fandom.com.s
  • Flame-based mobility: surrounding himself in flames to become temporarily invulnerable, then charging at Mega Man for devastating crashes

ABILITIES

  • Atomic Fire (Special Weapon) – A heat-based weapon powered by highly compressed nuclear energy. It can produce fire bursts at temperatures up to 12,000°C (21,632°F). Heat Man can charge it to unleash powerful fire blasts megaman.miraheze.org+1.
  • Fire Projection – Spits fire from his mouth and projects large fire blasts from his hands megaman.miraheze.org+1.
  • Flame Shield – Surrounds himself in flames to create an invulnerable barrier for a short time, then charges at opponents at high speed megaman.fandom.com+1.
  • Bodily Crash – A high-speed tackle attack that deals significant damage when he crashes into foes megaman.miraheze.org.
  • Contact Damage – Can inflict damage through physical contact with his flames or body
