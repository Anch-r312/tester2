TIPS

  • Look before you leap — Pits and gaps are deadly, so always check your path before jumping

  • Watch for enemy blocks — Some enemies will block your path mid-air; time your jumps carefully

  • Use the Metal Blade — Great for quick kills on Goblins, Kamanari Goros, Scworms, and Pipis

  • Avoid weapon reload pauses — If you pick up items, wait until the meter refills before jumping to

ROLE

  • Stage Boss: Air Man appears as the final boss of the Air Man stage, which takes place high above the clouds. The stage is a vertical, sky-based platforming challenge with hazards like falling pits, lightning-throwing Kamanari Goros, and wind-blowing Matasaburo

  • Combat Role: His main attack is generating powerful winds to push or pull enemies, create typhoons, and unleash rapid tornado barrages via the Air Shooter. This makes him a strong aerial and environmental hazard boss.

  • Gameplay Impact: Defeating Air Man is necessary to progress through the stage and unlock the next area. His stage is relatively short but dangerous, requiring careful movement and timing to avoid hazards

ABILITIES

  • Air Shooter – His Special Weapon, which fires rapid barrages of tornadoes. Can push enemies away, pull them or objects toward him, and create typhoons

  • Powerful Breeze – Blows away opposing core shields at the start of each turn and delays their regeneration Mega Man RPG Prototype.
